Squirrelplate

Designed and built for squirrels and other small clawed creatures by the squirrelfactory

Squirrelplate – a fluid template template which is designed to work on all screen sizes from tiny mobile up to large desktops. Ideally suited for creating simple, elegant web pages that focuses on content.

Squirrels belong to a large family of small or medium-sized rodents called the Sciuridae. The family includes tree squirrels, ground squirrels, chipmunks, marmots (including woodchucks), flying squirrels, and prairie dogs. Squirrels are indigenous to the Americas, Eurasia, and Africa and have been introduced to Australia. Squirrels are first attested in the Eocene, about 40 million years ago, and are most closely related to the mountain beaver and to the dormice among living species.

The Details

The concept of Squirrelplate is to create a really simple fluid template that focuses on content and how it adapts to the size of the screen. Go ahead, view the template on different sized screens.

  • Minimal css
  • No media queries
  • No complicated grid systems
